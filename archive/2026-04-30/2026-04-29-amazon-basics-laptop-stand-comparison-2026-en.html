Which One Should You Buy?

Buy the Aluminum Portable (B07GFF82DP) if:

  • You have a 13" or smaller laptop
  • You work from multiple locations (home, coffee shop, office)
  • Portability is your top priority
  • You want the most proven/popular Amazon Basics stand

Buy the Ergonomic Adjustable (B0BLRJ4R8F) if:

  • You have a 14"-15.6" laptop
  • You work from the same desk every day
  • You care about posture, neck alignment, and long-term comfort
  • You want to connect an external monitor and use the laptop as a secondary screen

Don't buy either if:

  • You already have a monitor at eye level — a laptop stand's main job is screen height, which a monitor solves
  • You need to run your laptop at full power under sustained load — even with ventilation, a stand is not active cooling

The Honest Assessment

The Aluminum Portable's 23,000+ reviews with 4.8 stars is the signal here. That's not marketing — it's real-world validation from a massive sample. For the vast majority of people who just need a laptop stand that works without fuss, this is the one.

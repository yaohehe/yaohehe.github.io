Which Amazon Basics Toaster Should You Buy

KitchenAmazon BasicsReviewSmall Appliances(This article contains affiliate links — I earn a small commission at no extra cost to you)

Bottom Line Up Front

Three Amazon Basics toaster models, three different audiences:

  • **2-Slice (900W, B072P11H8L)**: $21 — fine for singles or occasional use. Lowest power, takes ~3 min per batch, cannot do 4 slices at once. Not ideal for busy households.
  • **4-Slice (1600W, B07SW6SLKB)**: $35 — the sweet spot for most homes. Nearly double the power, can toast 4 slices simultaneously, has defrost and cancel buttons. Best value of the three.
  • **Air Fryer Oven (1500W, B0DL8TVRCT)**: $80 — Multi-function: air fryer + oven + toaster in one unit. 23QT capacity for 4-6 person households. If you already own an air fryer, this adds less value; if counter space is tight, it saves one appliance.

2-Slice Standard: Functional but Hits a Ceiling

900W is standard for this price tier. In practice:

Pros

  • Cheapest entry point at $21
  • 6 browning settings cover light toast to dark crunch
  • Compact footprint — fits even in a dorm mini-kitchen
  • Removable crumb tray makes cleanup easy

Cons

  • Cannot toast 4 slices at once — if you need that, go 4-slot
  • 900W means noticeably slower toasting compared to the 1600W model (~30% slower at same temperature)
  • No defrost function — frozen bread needs to thaw first
  • No cancel/lift button

Best for: Singles, weekend-only use, budget-first, very small kitchens

Skip if: You are in a rush in the morning, need to toast 4+ slices, have a household of 2+

Link: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B072P11H8L?tag=techpassive-20

---

4-Slice Classic: The Default Recommendation

The 1600W model is what I recommend to most readers. The 78% power increase is not just about speed — it changes the experience. You can toast 4 slices simultaneously on weekday mornings. No more splitting batches.

Pros

  • 1600W — fast toasting, handles 4 slices without breaking a sweat
  • Dual independent slots — toast 2 or 4, no waste
  • Defrost function: frozen bread goes in, fresh-textured toast comes out
  • Cancel/lift button: rescue your toast if you set it too dark
  • $35 price tag — strong value for what you get

Cons

  • Larger footprint than the 2-slot
  • Higher power = slightly higher electricity (less than $0.01 per use difference — not a real factor)
  • No air fryer capability

Best for: 2-4 person families, busy weekday mornings, daily use, practical buyers

Skip if: You only toast one slice occasionally, already own a good air fryer, very small kitchens

Link: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07SW6SLKB?tag=techpassive-20

---

Air Fryer Oven: Space Saver or Duplicate Appliance?

This is the odd one out — it is not really a toaster first. It is an air fryer that can also toast. 23QT capacity means you can fit a whole chicken. That is not a toaster; that is a counter-top oven.

Pros

  • Genuinely multi-function: air fry, bake, roast, toast — one machine instead of three
  • 23QT capacity handles 4-6 person meals
  • 1500W is sufficient for toast (about 2.5 min per batch)
  • Preset smart menus for beginners
  • Removable washable parts — easier to clean than most air fryers

Cons

  • $80 price tag — 4x the 2-slot, 2.3x the 4-slot
  • As an air fryer, it underperforms dedicated units (a $60 Ninja Air Fryer does the air frying better)
  • Takes significant counter space
  • Toast quality slightly less consistent than dedicated toasters (heat distribution is not as uniform)

Best for: Counter space is tight, want to replace both a toaster and an air fryer, 4+ person household, frequently roast whole chickens or large portions

Skip if: Primary use case is just toast, already have a good air fryer, counter space is already maxed out

Link: https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0DL8TVRCT?tag=techpassive-20

Final One-Line Verdicts

Buy the 2-slice if: Single, on a budget, mostly occasional use, dorm or office

Buy the 4-slice if: 2-4 person home, weekday mornings matter, use it 3+ times a week, want the best practical daily tool

Buy the air fryer oven if: Kitchen counter is sacred and you want to consolidate appliances, have both baking and air frying needs, 4+ person household, willing to pay a premium for multi-function

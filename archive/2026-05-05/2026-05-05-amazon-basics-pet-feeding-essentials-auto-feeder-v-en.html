2. Water Fountains: The Key to Getting Cats to Drink More

Amazon Basics Stainless Steel Fountain (~$25-35)

  • Capacity: 2L
  • Filtration: Charcoal filter, replace every 2-3 weeks
  • Material: Stainless steel interior, resists bacterial growth
  • Noise: Under 50dB, livable in living room

Why Cats Especially Need Moving Water

Cats' wild ancestors were desert animals—instinctively wary of stagnant water. A flowing water fountain mimics natural drinking scenarios. Real-world testing shows most cats increase water intake by 30-50%.

Competitor: Catit Fountain ($45)

  • Three water flow settings
  • Filter cartridges are universal
  • Amazon Basics saves you ~$10, with comparable core performance

Real User Feedback:

  • ✅ Stainless steel genuinely safer than plastic, no weird smell
  • ✅ Cats started drinking water voluntarily (most noticeable change)
  • ❌ Filter cartridges are consumables—ongoing cost
  • ❌ Water pump has subtle hum, may affect noise-sensitive cats

Best for: Cat households, especially male cats (prone to urinary issues, more water intake helps prevent this)

Why Kitchen Knives Deserve Careful Selection

Your kitchen knife is the tool you'll use most often. A good one makes prep work enjoyable; a bad one turns every potato into a chore.

Over 3 months, I tested knife sets ranging from $25 to $60, focusing on:

  • How long the edge stays sharp
  • Grip comfort and slip resistance
  • Balance and weight feel
  • Cleaning and maintenance ease

My testing protocol was straightforward: I used each knife set daily for meal prep, cutting 2-3 vegetables each weekday and a full dinner prep on weekends. I tracked sharpness changes at the 2-week, 4-week, and 8-week marks using a simple paper-shaving test — if the blade can't shave paper effortlessly, it's losing its edge.

Core Recommendations

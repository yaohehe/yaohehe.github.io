What to Look For: The Three Things That Matter in a Power Strip

When buying a power strip, three things matter most:

  • **Outlet count and spacing**: How many devices do you have? Do chargers block adjacent outlets?
  • **Surge protection**: Can it protect your devices from voltage spikes?
  • **USB charging ports**: Do you need separate chargers, or built-in USB?

Who Should Buy What

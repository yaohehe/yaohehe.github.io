Why Go Glass?

Glass kettles let you see the water level and boiling state at a glance — no guessing how much is left. They're also easier to clean (vinegar removes limescale buildup quickly). If you brew tea, pour-over coffee, or need precise water temperatures for baby formula, glass is the more intuitive choice.

Practical Tips Before You Buy

1. Limescale buildup: Glass kettles show water spots clearly — descale monthly with equal parts white vinegar and water, boil, then rinse thoroughly

2. Dry burning: All three have boil-dry protection, but don't make a habit of running them empty — it degrades the heating element over time

3. Voltage check: Confirm the model supports 220V for use in China (some US-only models are 110V only)

4. Price tracking: Use a price tracker or wait for Amazon's deal events — all three frequently drop 15-20% below their typical price

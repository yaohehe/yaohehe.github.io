Quick Verdict

Get the 5-piece hardware set if you need solid, wall-mounted bathroom hardware with a consistent metal finish across all pieces.

5-Piece Hardware Set (B07767BJG4)

  • Material: Zinc alloy base + stainless steel surface with Oil Rubbed Bronze finish
  • Finish: Matte texture that resists fingerprints well
  • Installation: Wall-mounted with screws and anchors (included)

6-Piece Accessory Set (B0DLGCZVSP)

  • Material: ABS body + ceramic pieces (soap dispenser and dish are ceramic)

In hand: The 5-piece set feels noticeably heavier and more premium. The 6-piece kit's ceramic soap dish is nice, but the ABS body has a definite plastic feel that may become brittle over time in a humid bathroom.

Installation Effort

Both require drilling, but differ in complexity:

5-piece set: Standard drillMount each piece separately. Includes clear diagram instructions. One person can finish all 5 pieces in about 30 minutes.

6-piece kit: Only 2-3 pieces need drilling; the soap dispenser and dish sit on the counter. However, counter space becomes a constraint — if your vanity is small, fitting everything neatly is tricky.

Practical tip: If you hate drilling into tile, use the 6-piece kit's countertop pieces and skip the wall-mounted ones.

Who Should Buy What

Get the 5-piece hardware set if you:

  • Are doing a full bathroom remodel or new construction

  • Value the matte metal finish over lightweight plastic
  • Don't mind drilling once for a long-term install

Get the 6-piece accessory kit if you:

  • Rent and want minimal wall damage
  • Like the Ivory white look and prefer warmer bathroom tones

Get neither if you:

  • Need adhesive-only solutions (rentals, shared walls)

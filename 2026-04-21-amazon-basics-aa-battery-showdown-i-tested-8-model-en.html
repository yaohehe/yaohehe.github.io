Key Things to Know Before You Buy

1. International Shipping Costs

Batteries are classified as hazardous goods for international shipping from Amazon US. Shipping runs $13-20 depending on quantity. Recommendations:

  • Buy bulk packs to amortize shipping cost across more cells
  • Split orders with friends to share shipping
  • Check Amazon Global or local equivalents (Amazon.cn, JD.com) for potentially lower total cost

2. Shelf Life Reality

Amazon Basics alkaline batteries are rated for 10-year leak-free storage. In my own testing, cells stored 3+ years showed only 5-8% capacity loss — essentially negligible.

3. Authenticity

Buying from Amazon directly (Fulfilled by Amazon / ships and sold by Amazon.com) means essentially zero counterfeit risk. Amazon Basics products are fulfilled directly by Amazon.

4. Returns

Amazon offers 30-day refund/replacement on defective items. If you receive batteries with obvious defects (leaking on arrival, severely underperforming), file a claim immediately.

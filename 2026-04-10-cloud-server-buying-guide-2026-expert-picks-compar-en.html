Critical Factors to Evaluate Before Buying

1. Performance Requirements

Calculate your actual needs before selecting a plan:

  • **CPU**: Count cores needed for peak load, consider burst requirements
  • **RAM**: Database-heavy apps need more memory; rule of thumb is 2GB minimum for production
  • **Storage**: NVMe SSDs are now standard; calculate growth projections
  • **Network**: Check bandwidth limits and whether egress (outbound data transfer) costs apply

2. Geographic Location Matters More Than Ever

Latency directly impacts user experience. In 2026, with global user expectations at an all-time high:

  • **Targeting US users**: Choose us-west, us-east, or Central regions
  • **European audience**: Frankfurt, Amsterdam, or London offer low latency
  • **Asia-Pacific**: Singapore, Tokyo, or Sydney provide regional coverage
  • **Multi-region strategy**: Consider CDN + multi-region deployment for global applications

3. Uptime Guarantees vs Reality

4. Hidden Costs That Surprise Buyers

Watch for these often-overlooked expenses:

  • **Egress traffic**: Inbound is usually free; outbound can be expensive at scale
  • **Snapshots and backups**: May incur additional storage charges
  • **Load balancers**: Often $15-25/month minimum
  • **Elastic IPs**: Some providers charge for unused static IPs
  • **API requests**: Microtransactions can add up for automation-heavy setups
